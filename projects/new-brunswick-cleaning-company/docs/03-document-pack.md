# Document Pack (What to Store in This Repo)

This file defines your core records. Keep file names consistent so audits and renewals are easier.

## Suggested repository storage layout

```text
projects/new-brunswick-cleaning-company/
  docs/
  records/
    registration/
    cra/
    permits/
    insurance/
    worksafenb/
    hr/
    clients/
    finance/
```

## 1) Registration records

Store in `records/registration/`:

- Business name registration confirmation
- Incorporation documents (if incorporated)
- Shareholder/director records (if applicable)
- Annual return filing confirmations

## 2) CRA and tax records

Store in `records/cra/`:

- BN confirmation
- GST/HST account setup confirmation
- Payroll account setup confirmation
- GST/HST filing summaries and proofs of payment
- Payroll remittance confirmations and year-end slips

## 3) Permit/licence records

Store in `records/permits/`:

- BizPaL result export/screenshot
- Municipal permit or licence confirmations
- Any zoning/home occupation approvals

## 4) Insurance records

Store in `records/insurance/`:

- Policy declarations (CGL, auto, property, etc.)
- Certificates of insurance (COI)
- Bond documents (if any)
- Renewal notices
- Claim correspondence

## 5) WorkSafeNB and safety records

Store in `records/worksafenb/`:

- Registration confirmation or voluntary coverage confirmation
- Payroll/assessment submissions
- Clearance confirmations (if needed)
- Incident/near-miss reports
- Safety meeting and training records

## 6) HR and employment records

Store in `records/hr/`:

- Job descriptions
- Signed offers/contracts
- Orientation/training checklists
- Time tracking records
- Payroll summaries

## 7) Client and sales records

Store in `records/clients/`:

- Signed service agreements
- Change orders/scope updates
- Site-specific instructions
- QA reports and issue logs

## 8) Finance records

Store in `records/finance/`:

- Invoices (issued)
- Payment reports (received)
- Expense receipts
- Monthly close summaries
- Basic KPI dashboard exports

## File naming convention (recommended)

Use:

`YYYY-MM-DD_category_client-or-vendor_short-description.ext`

Examples:

- `2026-05-02_registration_snb_business-name-confirmation.pdf`
- `2026-05-03_insurance_provider_cgl-policy-declaration.pdf`
- `2026-05-06_client_abc-property_signed-service-agreement.pdf`
